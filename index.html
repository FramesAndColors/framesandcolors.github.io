About Me

Hello! I'm Gourab Mondal, the creative director and lead professional behind Frames and Colors. My journey in visual arts spans over 13 years, dedicated entirely to honing the craft of photography and videography. My passion originated from a childhood fascination with capturing moments, evolving through studying the techniques and philosophies of masters like Ansel Adams and Henri Cartier-Bresson. This dedication fuels my commitment to providing a premier photography and videography service in Kolkata. My approach centers on capturing authentic human emotion and crafting compelling visual narratives through both candid photography and cinematic videography, ensuring your memories are preserved beautifully and genuinely for years to come.
